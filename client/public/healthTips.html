Healthy Lifestyle Tips

  • ✓ Engage in regular physical activity, like running, swimming, or cycling.
  • ✓ Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily.
  • ✓ Prioritize 7-8 hours of quality sleep every night.
  • ✓ Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • ✓ Limit the intake of processed foods and sugary drinks.
  • ✓ Manage stress through meditation, deep breathing, or yoga.
  • ✓ Regularly check-up with your healthcare provider.
